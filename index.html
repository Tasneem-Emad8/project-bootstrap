popular destination in Switzerland

Zurich is one of the most important and largest cities in Switzerland. It is located in the center of the north of the country, close to the German border on Lake Zurich. The city is famous for its banking companies, which are ranked the best in the world. The Swiss city of Zurich has been voted the best city to live in the world for three years in a row.

Zermatt is a small tourist village in southern Switzerland on the border with Italy. Zermatt is located in the Alps and is close to the famous Matterhorn, which is pictured on Toblerone chocolate wrappers. The village is home to more than 5,000 people and is more than 1,600 meters above sea level.

Basel is a city in northwestern Switzerland on the Rhine River. Basel is the third largest city in Switzerland, with a population of 177,595 within the city limits. The official language in Basel is Swiss Standard German, and the main spoken language is the local Basel German dialect.
